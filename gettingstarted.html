Programming & Microprocessors

Gaining more control over all the details.

Processing

A Java-based programming language that caters towards graphics and graphics-related libraries. Check out Processing.

Arduino

A microcontroller with a great programming system behind it - with integration with other languages like Processing. Check out the Arduino board.

Other boards

Other boards that follow along the lines of Arduino are the Intel Atom Board, the Raspberry Pi, and the Sparkfun Red Board... all branded a bit differently, but technically able to all do the same thing.

Integration with sensors & more

Once you can program and complete circuits with given parts, you can experiment with buying more parts from places like Sparkfun which could be anything from motors and photocells to DS touch screens, and you can play with taking apart toys and old computers and see what you can create!
